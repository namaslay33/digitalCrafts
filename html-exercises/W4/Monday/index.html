~ Welcome ~
Thai Bistro is a family owned business. The restaurant been around for over 5 years serving the Houston community with authentic, delicious Thai food. Currently, there are two locations: 1) the West University area near Buffalo Speedway and 2)the West Chase area in the Royal Oaks Plaza.
